Augmented Reality
Augmented Reality is the blending of Virtual Reality and real life. AR is using technology to superimpose information such as sounds, images, and text on the real world that we can see.
AR is not a new technology. It was used in display panels of fighter aircraft in the 1990s. In 2009, Massachusetts Institute of Technology developed SixthSense ; a small device worn like a pendant, and combines the use of camera, projector, smartphone and mirror.

Virtual Reality
In Virtual Reality , we create a virtual world that users can interact with. In this world, the user cannot easily distinguish between what is real and virtual.
In both VR/AR, people must wear HMD (Head Mounted Displays) or a VR headset like Oculus Rfit or the Google Cardboard. Both AR & VR provide a tool immersive experience to the user. This technology is used in education. Imagine being in a historical battle, you can really feel the battle and touch the organs.
